§01 · The Case for Change05 / 18
The Old Model
100,000 SNT at $100 was over-allocated
and structurally exposed to GPU cost abuse.
GPU Cost Exposure
The fMRI layer is expensive to run.
The Simulated fMRI modality (TRIBE v2) requires a 28–32 GB VRAM cluster. At 100K SNT, a user could run ~66 complete 15s fMRI scans per month — far more compute than the $100 price point can sustainably cover.
Usage Pattern Mismatch
Agencies test in bursts, not every day.
A flat 100K token pool was misaligned with the actual workflow. Most freelancers needed high-frequency lightweight checks — not a surplus of deep fMRI runs they'd never use.
Broken Upgrade Path
No reason to move to a higher tier.
With 100K SNT available, the majority of freelancers could operate indefinitely on the $100 plan. The expansion revenue path to the $420 tier was blocked by over-allocation at entry.
Structural Subsidy
Heavy users were cross-subsidised.
Power users running intensive fMRI scans were effectively being subsidised by lighter users. This erodes platform margins at exactly the tier designed to acquire and convert new customers.

§02 · New Pricing Architecture07 / 18
The Standard Value Metric
$1.00 USD = 1,000 SNT
SNT (Stimulus Neuro-Tokens) is the platform's native billing currency. One transparent conversion rate. No per-seat fees. No resolution tiers. You pay for the cognitive modalities you activate and the duration of the clip you scan — nothing else.
Eye Gaze · Visual Salience
10 SNT/sec
Low compute. 2D CNN saliency on normalised 224×224 frames. Predicts where the eye lands in the first 250ms. Ideal for visual hierarchy and thumb-stop checks.
Facial Expression · Emotion AI
15 SNT/sec
Moderate GPU. Frame-by-frame facial Action Unit parsing. Tracks micro-expression engagement and emotional arousal valleys across the clip timeline.
Voice Sentiment · Acoustics
15 SNT/sec
Moderate GPU. Dual-recurrent encoder processes audio and speech simultaneously. Extracts acoustic pitch, resonance, and confidence vectors in real time.
Simulated fMRI Projection
60 SNT/sec
High compute. Loads Meta's TRIBE v2 trimodal model across 28–32 GB VRAM. Maps emotional reward (amygdala) and processing friction (prefrontal cortex) — the deepest diagnostic available.

§02 · New Pricing Architecture10 / 18
Why This Pricing
Enough to work.
Not enough to waste. The Freelancer tier was specifically re-engineered from 100K → 40K SNT. Here is why.
01
The fMRI Problem: 100K SNT covered 66 deep scans at the entry price point.
The fMRI modality costs 60 SNT/sec. At 100,000 SNT, a user could run 1,111 seconds of pure fMRI inference — equivalent to ~66 complete 15s scans per month. Serving those scans on NVIDIA A100 clusters at $100 was a structural loss.
66 → 20
02
The New Balance: 40K SNT maps precisely to how Indian freelancers actually work.
Indian performance freelancers managing ₹20K–₹45K monthly SaaS budgets need 8–9 visual variants tested every day — not deep brain scans. 40K SNT gives them 266 gaze checks/month (daily usage) plus 20 fMRI runs as a buffer for premium corporate work.
8–9/day
03
The Upgrade Path: Volume pushes users toward Booster Packs or the $420 tier naturally.
When a freelancer wins a corporate pitch requiring 10+ fMRI runs, they hit their token ceiling. At that point, Agency Booster Packs ($10 / 10,000 SNT) become the bridge — or they upgrade to Growth Agency, generating predictable expansion revenue (NRR target: >125%).
>125%

60–80% of IDFA-based attribution is now suppressed. Pre-launch neural validation is the only scalable way to guarantee ad ROAS when post-launch signals are unreliable.

Layer 3 · Value Proposition & Positioning
The job isn't to test content.
It's to stop burning the media budget. Three jobs, one platform.
Functional Job
"Stop the thumb."
"I need to know if our 15-second ad hook will stop a user's thumb in under 0.3 seconds — before I waste our client's media budget." The scroll-stop probability in the first 0.3s is worth more than any post-launch A/B test.
Emotional Job
"Pitch with proof, not opinion."
"I want to walk into a client meeting with a brain scan that says why Creative B wins — not a creative director's gut. I want scientific confidence, not a debate." Stimulus turns subjectivity into a defensible number.
Operational Job
"Cut 12 hours to 2."
"I need to reduce our content testing and revision loop from 12–18 hours down to under 2 hours." Entropik reports 60% reduction in time-to-insight. Stimulus targets the same benchmark, accessible at ₹12 per scan.

Layer 4 · Go-to-Market Motion
Three channels.
One compounding flywheel.
Channel Mix
How we reach them
MCP Server (Developer-first): API integrations, Figma plugin, Chrome extension. Lets AI agents autonomously call Stimulus scans — turns the platform into infrastructure, not just a tool.
D2C PLG: ₹99 pay-per-scan self-serve portal with UPI checkout. Zero-friction entry point. No demo required. First scan in 60 seconds.
Agency Partner Program: Direct enterprise sales with 2 Transformer beachhead agencies (Social Panga, ROI Minds) as anchor logos and case study sources.
Demand Generation
How we create pull
"Comment BRAIN" Campaign: Viral loop on LinkedIn and Instagram. Post a scan result publicly → invite others to scan their own content. Makes brain heatmaps shareable social proof.
"Cyber Cafe" Activation: Offline B2B activation in Gurugram — walk into an agency's office, scan their latest creative live, show results in 60 seconds. High-conversion, high-PR tactic.
Scarcity Reels: Anonymised before/after scan comparisons of real campaigns shared publicly. Shows what the brain sees vs. what agencies assumed.
Sales Motion
How we convert & expand
PLG-to-PLS Trigger: When a user hits 80% of their monthly token allocation, HubSpot automatically initiates a sales cadence — upgrade offer, case study, and a call to a Growth PM.
Funnel: Free trial → ₹99 pay-per-scan → Freelancer $100 → Growth Agency $420. Each step is triggered by usage behaviour, not a sales call.
Territory Sequence: Bangalore first (91% AI adoption), then Mumbai (87%), then Tier 2 hubs (Ahmedabad 68%, Jaipur 61%).
